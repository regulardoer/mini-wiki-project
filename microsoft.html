About Microsoft

Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. The company became a pioneer in personal computing through the development of the Windows operating system.

Today, Microsoft operates across multiple sectors including cloud computing, enterprise software, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and gaming.

As one of the world's most valuable technology companies, Microsoft plays a central role in digital infrastructure and global enterprise solutions.

Main Products and Services

  • Windows: The world's leading desktop operating system.
  • Microsoft Office: Productivity tools used globally.
  • Azure: A major cloud computing platform.
  • Xbox: Gaming consoles and digital entertainment services.
  • LinkedIn: Professional networking platform.

Through these services, Microsoft supports businesses, governments, and individuals worldwide.

Global Influence

Microsoft's cloud platform, Azure, competes directly with Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud, positioning the company as a key player in global digital infrastructure.

The company continues to invest heavily in artificial intelligence, sustainability initiatives, and enterprise innovation.
