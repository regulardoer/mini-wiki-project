About Amazon

Amazon was founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos as an online bookstore. It quickly expanded into one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world.

Today, Amazon operates across e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, artificial intelligence, and logistics.

The company has transformed global retail and digital infrastructure through innovation and large-scale operations.

Main Products and Services

  • Amazon Marketplace: Global online shopping platform.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Leading cloud computing provider.
  • Prime: Subscription-based premium services.
  • Kindle: Digital reading devices and ebooks.
  • Alexa: AI-powered voice assistant.

Through these services, Amazon serves millions of customers, businesses, and developers worldwide.

Global Impact

Amazon Web Services dominates the cloud computing market, powering startups, enterprises, and governments globally.

Amazon continues to expand into AI, robotics, logistics automation, and entertainment streaming services.
